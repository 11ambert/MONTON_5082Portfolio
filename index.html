Resort Website

This website for Puting Bonbon Beach Resort is my creation! I designed it to showcase the beauty of their beach and the experiences they offer for guests.

Puting Bonbon Beach Resort


Home Page

Puting Bonbon Beach Resort's homepage entices visitors with a stunning image of their white sand beach and crystal-clear waters. The text emphasizes affordability and creating unforgettable memories, while the navigation bar allows easy access to details about cottages, room rates, reservation options, and the resort's background.



Cottages

Puting Bonbon Beach Resort's "Cottages" page caters to various group sizes and budgets. They offer a Function Hall for grand gatherings, standard Cottages for smaller groups, and even a shaded Table area for solo travelers or intimate picnics. Prices for day trips and overnight stays are displayed, along with a convenient "Reserve Here" button for each option.


Reservations

Absolutely, the design of your reservation page on the image adheres to a minimalist principle, making it straightforward and user-friendly. Key information like arrival date, number of guests and room type are all clearly labelled. With minimal elements on the screen, users can focus on filling out the form to secure their reservation quickly.



About

The "About Us" page adopts a minimalist design to ensure clear communication. Text takes center stage, describing the resort's mission to provide relaxation and unforgettable memories for various guests. While lacking complex imagery, the page effectively utilizes a hero text introduction, a detailed description of their offerings, and a strategic call to action button to "Explore Our Paradise," likely leading users to delve deeper into the specifics of the resort's amenities and activities.
